Our Goals

Reduced external ‘single’ dependencies
(shared is not bad!)

No desktop based, monolithic tools

Everything in the cloud

All IHTSDO services managed/controlled in house, although not necessarily hosted in house

Reusable & documented infrastructure and design promoting growth and innovation

Wide(r) pool of terminology tooling development resources

Providing the opportunity for a mature Managed Service product covering all domains/lines of business, for new (& existing) Members, supported by mature online tools

Tooling Roadmamp

2014 and onwards

Prioritization

  1. Members' key, clear, documented priorities
  2. Organization requirements
  3. Gaps or deficiencies in current tooling
  4. Services that already exist, but are provided by Members

'First-pass' core requirements are driven and implemented by the organization until a point of sufficient maturity

The output is presented to Members and the services evolves with Member input

Ultimately, all tooling development is driven to meet the requirements of Members with the efficient, high quality production of SNOMED CT on a regular and frequent basis.
