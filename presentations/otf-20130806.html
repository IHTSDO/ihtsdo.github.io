The IHTSDO Terminology Open Tooling Framework

...or the OTF

Tooling is a business and organisation wide topic

Tooling should only exist to meet business needs

Tooling must be an enabler, not an obstacle

The framework is a set of modules with standardized application programming interfaces (APIs)

that define fundamental software services to be consumed by business applications,
