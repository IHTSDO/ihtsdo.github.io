RESTful Interfaces

Why?

or rather why not SOAP?

RESTful Design Principles/Contraints

  • Client/Server approach
  • Stateless
  • Uniform interface
  • Layered
  • Backwards Compatibility
  • Idempotency (just about)
  • Defined in terms of HTTP methods (POST, GET, DELETE, PUT)
